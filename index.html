Family Batch Cook & Planner

Strategic prep for weeknight dinners and packed lunches

A few things worth calling out for the week:

  • Monday through Thursday front-loads the high EPA+DHA meals (salmon, sardines, mackerel) so you're getting direct anti-inflammatory omega-3 early in the week.
  • Friday's chia oats round it out with ALA-rich plant sources, which is a lighter finish to the week.
  • The Sunday batch cook is the real force multiplier — roasting the sweet potato and steaming the broccoli once means Thursday's bento literally takes 5 minutes to pack.
  • The quinoa batch carries Monday and Wednesday, and the lemon-EVOO-turmeric dressing works across three meals, so you make it once.

Total Sunday prep time is realistically 60–75 minutes including cleanup, and then each night-before task is 5–10 minutes.
